# Ban Hunter

This page is used to show hunters that are banned on [BP Window](../fronted-bp.md).
![禁用监管者](images/禁用监管者.png)

2 Current Ban and 3 Global Ban are preset.

#### Character Selector
Character selector is a Custom Control, which is a combination of a selection box, a confirm button and an image box. The selection is used to retrieve and select characters. Press Tab/Enter to confirm the choice. 

Simultaenously, this control also exists in the form of a selection box only. You will see it in the "Select Character" page.

> You can input "bb" to search for Guard 26
